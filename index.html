Cormorant Bird

Interesting Facts About Cormorants birdfacts@example.com

Cormorants are fascinating birds known for their diving abilities and distinctive appearance.

  • Cormorants are excellent swimmers, using their webbed feet to dive for fish.
  • They often spread their wings out to dry after diving, a characteristic pose.

Bird Facts

Diving Masters

Cormorants can dive to impressive depths to catch fish, staying underwater for up to a minute.

Wing-Drying Behavior

After diving, cormorants spread their wings to dry, as they do not have waterproof feathers like some other waterbirds.
